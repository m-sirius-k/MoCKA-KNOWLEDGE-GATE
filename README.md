# MoCKA Knowledge Gate

MoCKA Knowledge Gate is the institutional memory layer of the MoCKA Insight System.  
It organizes research reasoning, decisions, and verification context so that knowledge can be reused in future research.

Knowledge Gate receives reasoning traces and logs generated in the MoCKA Core and integrates results verified by the Transparency Layer.  
By structuring these contexts, it preserves research knowledge in a form that can be reused and extended.

Knowledge Gate therefore functions as the long-term research memory of the MoCKA ecosystem and acts as a bridge between past research and future institutional development.

---

# System Architecture

The MoCKA Insight System consists of several layers that connect research execution, verification, memory, and reuse.

- **MoCKA Core** — research execution and generation of primary outputs  
- **Knowledge Gate** — institutional memory and reasoning context storage  
- **Transparency Layer** — audit and verification layer  
- **External Brain** — integration with external knowledge systems  
- **Civilization Layer** — governance philosophy and institutional design  

Knowledge Gate sits within this structure as the layer responsible for preserving research context and transmitting knowledge into the future.

---

## MoCKA Civilization System Map

The overall structure of the MoCKA Insight System can be understood as a flow of research civilization.

Research begins from the Civilization layer, connects to external knowledge through the External Brain,  
executes experiments through the MoCKA Core, produces Evidence,  
is verified through the Transparency Layer, and is finally preserved by the Knowledge Gate.

This flow is not merely a processing pipeline but a structural representation of research civilization.

# Research Knowledge Flow

Knowledge Gate sits at the center of the MoCKA knowledge cycle.

Research does not end with a single result.  
Instead, it becomes part of a continuous cycle where knowledge is verified, institutionalized, and reused.

The fundamental research cycle can be summarized as follows:

1. **Execution** — research is executed by the MoCKA Core  
2. **Verification** — results are validated by the Transparency Layer  
3. **Institutionalization** — research context is structured and stored by Knowledge Gate  
4. **Reuse and Governance** — preserved knowledge supports future research and governance

Through this cycle, the MoCKA Insight System evolves as an accumulating research civilization.

---

## Knowledge Generation Cycle

In the MoCKA system, knowledge generation follows a cyclical model rather than a linear pipeline.

Execution produces results,  
Verification evaluates them,  
Institutionalization records them as institutional memory,  
and Reuse enables them to influence future research.

Knowledge Gate is the layer that ensures this cycle retains context and continuity.

# Knowledge Gate Infrastructure

Knowledge Gate operates in coordination with multiple supporting systems.

- **Experiment Registry** — experiment execution logs  
- **Verification System** — validation and audit infrastructure  
- **Research Map** — structured research documentation  
- **Repository Ecosystem** — storage of code and research artifacts  
- **AI Agents** — reasoning support and external knowledge access  

These systems surround the knowledge generation cycle and provide the institutional infrastructure that enables research continuity.

## System Architecture（MoCKA 全体の中での位置づけ）

- **MoCKA Core**：研究実行と一次成果物の生成

## Research Knowledge Flow（知識の流れ）

Knowledge Gate は MoCKA の知識循環の中心にあります。

研究は一度きりの成果として終わるのではなく、検証と制度化を経て再利用されることで、継続的に発展していきます。

基本的な研究循環は次のように整理できます。

1. **Execution**：MoCKA Core が研究を実行し、推論やログを生成する  
2. **Verification**：Transparency Layer が内容を検証する  
3. **Institutionalization**：Knowledge Gate が文脈を整理し制度的記憶として保存する  
4. **Reuse and Governance**：保存された知識が次の研究や制度設計に活用される  

この循環により、MoCKA Insight System は積み上がる研究文明として進化します。

Knowledge Gate はこの循環の中で、研究の文脈を制度的記憶として保存し、次の研究に引き継ぐ役割を担います。
